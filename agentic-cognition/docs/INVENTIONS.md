# AgenticCognition — 24 Inventions

## Tier 1: The Living Mirror (P0)

### 1. Living User Model
A breathing model with heartbeat, metabolism, and lifecycle stages. Not a static profile but an evolving entity.

### 2. Belief Graph
All beliefs exist in relationship. Supports, contradicts, requires, implies. Keystone beliefs hold everything together.

### 3. Decision Fingerprint
Your unique decision-making signature across 8 axes: information appetite, risk tolerance, speed/accuracy tradeoff, intuition/analysis balance, social influence, time horizon, emotional regulation, reversibility seeking.

### 4. Soul Reflection
Discovers the essence beneath the surface: core traits, fundamental drives, deep fears, and the gap between presented and authentic self.

## Tier 2: Belief Physics (P1)

### 5. Belief Crystallization
Beliefs harden over time. Old beliefs resist change with modification energy proportional to crystallization level.

### 6. Self-Concept Topology
Maps confidence peaks, insecurity valleys, blind canyons, defended territories, and growing edges.

### 7. Belief Drift
Tracks slow movement in belief positions over time with evidence, social, and emotional vectors.

### 8. Preference Oracle
Predicts preferences based on value alignment, belief resonance, and decision fingerprint.

## Tier 3: Shadow Psychology (P2)

### 9-13. Shadow Beliefs, Projections, Blindspots, Bias Field, Emotional Triggers
Maps the unconscious landscape: beliefs held but not admitted, traits projected onto others, systematic blind areas, cognitive biases, and emotional triggers with response patterns.
