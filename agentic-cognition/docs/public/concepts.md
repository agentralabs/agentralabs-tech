# Core Concepts

AgenticCognition is built on five foundational concepts that together form a living model of human consciousness.

## Living User Model

A continuously evolving representation of a human mind. The model has lifecycle stages (Birth, Infancy, Growth, Maturity, Crisis, Rebirth) and accumulates evidence through observations.

## Belief Physics

Beliefs are not static strings. They have physical properties: confidence, crystallization, entanglement, and conviction gravity. These properties govern how beliefs interact, evolve, and resist change over time.

## Shadow Psychology

The shadow is the part of the psyche operating below conscious awareness. AgenticCognition detects shadow beliefs, projections, blindspots, bias fields, and emotional triggers that the person cannot see about themselves.

## Drift Tracking

Longitudinal monitoring of how beliefs, values, and identity shift over time. Value tectonics tracks slow movement of core value systems. Growth rings preserve archaeological layers of who the person was at each stage.

## Prediction Engine

The preference oracle, decision simulation, and future projection systems use the full belief graph to predict reactions, choices, and identity trajectories before they happen.
