# Primary Problem Coverage

AgenticCognition addresses four primary problems that current AI systems fail to solve.

## Belief Drift Blindness

Current AI has no mechanism to detect when a user's beliefs, values, or convictions are silently shifting over time. AgenticCognition's drift tracking with value tectonics monitors how convictions shift, harden, or erode session over session, alerting when core beliefs approach instability thresholds.

## Decision Pattern Ignorance

AI responds to individual decisions without recognizing recurring patterns. AgenticCognition's decision fingerprinting and belief graph analysis detect recurring patterns, biases, risk tolerance, time horizon preferences, and cognitive blind spots that shape every choice the user makes.

## Shadow Belief Blindness

Users hold beliefs they would deny, and current AI cannot surface them. AgenticCognition's shadow psychology maps projections, defended regions, emotional triggers, and blindspots that operate below conscious awareness, giving the user visibility into their own hidden patterns.

## Identity Continuity Loss

Every conversation starts fresh. The AI has no persistent understanding of who the user is or who they are becoming. AgenticCognition's `.acog` files preserve the living user model across restarts, model switches, and long gaps, maintaining continuity of understanding across the entire relationship.

## Coverage Matrix

Each primary problem is addressed by multiple inventions across the five tiers (P0 through P4), ensuring deep coverage rather than surface-level fixes.
