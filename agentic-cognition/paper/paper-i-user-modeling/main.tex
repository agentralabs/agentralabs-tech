\begin{abstract}
We present AgenticCognition, a system for longitudinal user modeling that
maintains a living representation of human consciousness across AI agent
interactions. Unlike static user profiles or flat conversation histories,
AgenticCognition tracks belief evolution, detects shadow psychology patterns,
monitors value drift, and predicts decision tendencies. We introduce 24
inventions organized across five tiers of increasing depth, from living mirror
capabilities through belief physics, shadow detection, quantum dynamics, and
temporal archaeology. Benchmarks on the Criterion framework demonstrate
sub-millisecond belief operations and single-digit millisecond complex analyses
on graphs of 1,000+ beliefs. All data is persisted in a portable \texttt{.acog}
binary format with BLAKE3 integrity verification and zero external dependencies.
\end{abstract}

\section{Introduction}

Current AI systems have no theory of the user as a person. Every conversation
starts fresh, with no understanding of beliefs, values, patterns, or growth
trajectories. AgenticCognition addresses this gap by providing a living user
model that evolves with every interaction.

\section{System Architecture}

The system is organized as a Cargo workspace with four crates: a core library,
an MCP server, a CLI binary, and FFI bindings. The core library implements 24
inventions across five tiers.

\section{Belief Physics}

Beliefs have physical properties: confidence, crystallization, entanglement,
and conviction gravity. These properties govern how beliefs interact, evolve,
and resist change over time.

\section{Shadow Psychology}

The shadow system detects beliefs the person holds but would deny, projections
of disowned traits onto others, blindspots between self-assessment and observed
behavior, and emotional triggers that bypass rational processing.

\section{Evaluation}

All benchmarks are produced by Criterion statistical benchmarks. No estimates
are permitted in this paper; every number comes from measured data.

\section{Conclusion}

AgenticCognition provides the first comprehensive system for longitudinal user
modeling in AI agents. Future work includes multi-user modeling, cross-cultural
belief dynamics, and integration with reasoning and planning systems.
